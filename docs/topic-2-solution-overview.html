The Solution

Replace custom integration projects with a configurable rating orchestration platform.

InsuRateConnect is the layer between RQI platforms and rating engines that carriers are building from scratch today — over and over again.

A platform-agnostic orchestration layer that translates, routes, and governs every rating transaction between any source system and any rating engine — configured by the business, not built by developers.

How it answers each challenge
Challenge 1 — RQI Integration Gap

Platform-agnostic by design

Works with Guidewire, Duck Creek, Majesco — or any RQI platform. The source system is a configuration choice, not a code dependency. Switch platforms without rebuilding the integration.

Challenge 2 — Hidden Integration Tax

Configure instead of build

What used to take a full dev team months of custom service development is now a configuration exercise. Product onboarding drops from months to weeks. No custom code to build, test, or maintain.

Challenge 3 — Data & Format Mismatch

Built-in translation engine

140+ field mappings with 17 transformation types, JSON/XML/SOAP format conversion, and AI-assisted field suggestions. The platform handles the translation — field by field, format by format.

Challenge 4 — Preserving Rating Engine Investment

Engine-agnostic — connect to anything

Earnix, CGI Ratabase, homegrown actuarial models — the target engine is a configuration, not architecture. Carriers keep the rating engines they've invested in. The platform adapts to the engine, not the other way around.

Challenge 5 — Actuarial Teams Locked Out

Business users own the rating logic

Actuaries define surcharges, discounts, and state-specific rules directly — in plain English via AI or through a structured editor. Scopes let them tag rules to specific states, coverages, and transaction types. No JIRA ticket, no dev sprint, no deployment cycle. A regulatory rule change goes from business decision to live configuration in hours.

One platform. Any source. Any engine. Configured by the team that understands the business.
